First

You'll need to cut the body into pieces. Best to chop the limbs, the body, and the head into different parts. I suggest splitting the legs in half at the knees, since it makes it easier to put away. Remember to cut at the joint, where the connetion between bones are the weakest.

Second

Second, grab a few garbage bags, and put two limbs in each bag. Remember to stuff it as to not raise any suspicion. The body goes in one seperate bag.
